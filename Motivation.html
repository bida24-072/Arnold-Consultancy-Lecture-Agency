Strategic Leadership in Digital Environments

Lecture Overview

In today’s evolving digital economy, leadership requires strategic thinking and the ability to drive innovation through remote collaboration. This group session will guide professionals through case studies and best practices in managing distributed teams, enhancing virtual communication, and maintaining culture in hybrid workspaces.
