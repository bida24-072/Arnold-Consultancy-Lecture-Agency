Voice Command Demo

Try saying commands like:

  • "Scroll down" or "Scroll up"
  • "Go to top" or "Go to bottom"
  • "Change theme" (light/dark)
  • "Search for [your query]"
  • "Read content"
  • "Stop listening"

Available Voice Commands:

  • Navigation: "go home", "scroll up/down", "go to top/bottom"
  • Appearance: "dark mode", "light mode", "bigger text", "normal text"
  • Content: "read content", "pause reading", "search for [term]"
  • Control: "start listening", "stop listening", "help"
